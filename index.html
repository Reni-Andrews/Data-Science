👋 Welcome to the Department of Data Science

Explore our vision, mission, faculty, and educational excellence

🌟 Vision

To become a center of excellence in Data Science education and research, producing globally competent professionals and researchers.

🎯 Mission

  • Provide quality education in Data Science and related fields.
  • Encourage innovation, research, and problem-solving skills.
  • Collaborate with industries and research organizations.
  • Promote ethical practices and lifelong learning.

🔍 Overview

The Department offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs focusing on machine learning, big data analytics, artificial intelligence, and deep learning, preparing students for careers in the tech industry and academia.

📝 Message from the HoD

"Welcome to the Department of Data Science. We are committed to providing cutting-edge education and fostering a research-driven environment. Our faculty members are actively engaged in pioneering research and industry collaborations."
